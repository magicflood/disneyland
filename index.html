Your questions... answered!

Can I buy Disney Park tickets at the entrance of the Disney Parks?
Disney Park tickets cannot be purchased at the entrance to the Disney Parks, except in certain cases. See details and prices here.

​Tickets can be purchased directly at the entrance to the Disney Parks only when paying with holiday vouchers, gift cards or cash and provided that the Disney Parks have not reached maximum capacity for the day of your visit. Guests who arrive at the entrance to the Disney Parks without a valid ticket purchased in advance may be denied access if the maximum capacity has been reached. The maximum capacity is based on the number of visitors that the Disney Parks can accommodate while maintaining satisfactory conditions. This limit depends on factors such as the number of attractions available and the Park opening times on the day, in order to ensure the best possible experience for all our Guests.
Do I need to register my visit dates before my visit?
If you purchase an undated Disney Park ticket you must register your visit date in advance to enter the Disney Parks on the date of your choice.
Which methods of payment are accepted at Disneyland Paris?

Conditions

(1) What are the exchange and refund conditions for Dated tickets 1-4 days?
Dated tickets 1-4 days can be cancelled and refunded up to 3 days before your chosen date (including the day of arrival, for example, if you have a ticket for 4 June, you have until midnight on 1 June to cancel).

You can cancel your ticket online directly from the "My Bookings" section in your Disney Account.

If we specified that your booking can only be cancelled by email, you must send your names, 8-digit booking reference, visit date, a photo of your tickets (with the barcode clearly visible) or your PDF ticket, to the following address: refund.ticket@disneylandparis.com. We will refund your tickets as soon as possible, via the same payment method used at the moment of booking. If you would like to change your visit date, you must cancel your existing tickets and purchase new tickets for a different visit date (subject to availability when booking). It is not possible to change your visit date by e-mail.
(3) Reserved viewing area conditions
With reserved viewing area, you can enjoy the show or parade from a dedicated zone at the selected date and time, unless it is impossible for the show or parade to take place. This paid option is limited in quantity and subject to availability. ​

​In unforeseen circumstances, such as poor weather conditions or technical issues, our shows and parade may be modified or cancelled without prior notice.​
(4) Guaranteed access conditions
Paid service, subject to availability. With guaranteed access, you can attend the show at the selected time, unless for any reason the show is cancelled, for example due to poor weather conditions.
